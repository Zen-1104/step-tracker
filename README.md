# Step Tracker

A Garmin Connect IQ app for the Vivoactive 5 that tracks your daily steps with customizable exclusion windows.

## What it does

Step Tracker works like the native Garmin step counter but lets you define up to 3 time frames where steps are **not counted**. This is useful if you want to track only the steps you take during specific parts of your day — for example, excluding your commute or work hours.

## Features

- Tracks daily steps from midnight, just like the native Garmin counter
- Up to 3 customizable exclusion time windows
- Visual arc progress toward your step goal
- Manual pause and resume
- Set your own step goal
- Clean, minimal interface

## How to use

1. Install the app on your Vivoactive 5
2. Open the Connect IQ app on your phone
3. Go to the app settings and set your exclusion windows in HHMM format (e.g. 630 for 6:30 AM, 1830 for 18:30)
4. Set your daily step goal directly on the watch
5. The step count will automatically pause during your exclusion windows and resume when they end

## Privacy Policy

Step Window does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of your device. All data is processed and stored locally on your device only. No data is shared with third parties. No internet connection is required or used.
